Accepted Papers

We are pleased to announce the papers accepted for the Workshop on Agentic AI for Visual Media. These papers represent the latest advances in autonomous visual content creation, multimodal AI systems, and agent-based approaches to computer vision tasks.

The accepted papers will be presented during the workshop and will contribute to the growing body of knowledge in this emerging field. Each paper has undergone rigorous peer review to ensure high-quality contributions to the workshop.
